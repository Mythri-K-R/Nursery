About Balaji Nursery

Rooted in Tiptur, we help farmers plant with confidence.

Balaji Nursery supplies healthy grafted fruit plants and trusted guidance for orchard planning. From variety selection to spacing and first-year care, our team supports growers with practical, field-tested advice.

True-to-Type Plants

Each plant is sourced and raised with strict variety checks, so growers get what they pay for.

Farmer-First Service

We guide first-time and experienced farmers with practical support through every planting season.

Climate-Suited Selection

Recommendations are based on local weather, water availability, and market demand in your area.

Responsible Growing

We promote balanced nutrition, soil health, and long-term orchard productivity.

Our Plantation Services

Everything needed to start and maintain a productive orchard.

Orchard Planning

Site layout, spacing guidance, and crop combination planning for long-term farm performance.

Plant Health Advisory

On-Call Farmer Support

Quick support for disease concerns, replanting questions, and field-level troubleshooting.
