Macadamia Grafted Elite is recommended for farmers planning long-term orchard investment. Grafted plants provide better uniformity and predictable growth compared to seedling material.

At Balaji Nursery, each batch is selected for stem strength, healthy root zone, and graft union quality. We also provide spacing and nutrition guidance for first-year establishment.

  • Early establishment with proper staking and irrigation.
  • Suitable for mixed orchard strategy with select intercrops in initial years.
  • Guidance support available during first season after planting.

Recommended Spacing
7m x 7m or as per field slope and irrigation design.

Soil Preference
Well-drained loamy soil with good organic matter and no prolonged water stagnation.

Water Requirement
Regular irrigation in summer; drip is recommended for efficient moisture management.
